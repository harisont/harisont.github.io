## Books
I read all sorts of stuff this year.
Interestingly, all three books that impacted me the most were (arguably) classics, and two of them I had already read in high school.
Other than these, the comics were all quite good and most of the nonfiction was definitely worth reading.
The literature? 
A bit more hit-or-miss.
